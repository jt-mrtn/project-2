About  JT

Hi – nice to e-meet you! 😉 I work at the Washington Conservatory of Music where I assist in helping kids & adults learn to play an instrument, participate in ensembles, and all the wonderful things that go with that. Check us out, we're in a National Park!

I began my career as a professional musician. I play classical and Irish traditional styles on flutes and whistles, and do my best at the piano. Some recordings of me on flute are on YouTube. I'm also frequently found on dirt trails on my Gary Fisher mountain bike.

Every now and then, curiosity and various job-related tasks led me to explore computers more and more. Eventually, I grew more enthusiastic about the digital world enough to land a job as a report writer. This led to software developing at the Smithsonian. In becoming a programmer, I realized how similar it is to playing in an ensemble.

Recently, I sought out Thinkful and Kadenze to hone my skills further and discovered I liked the creative side of coding the best. Check out my Projects !
